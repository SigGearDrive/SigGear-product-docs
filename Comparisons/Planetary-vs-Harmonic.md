# Planetary vs Harmonic Drive for Robotics

## Introduction

Planetary gearboxes and harmonic drives are two of the most widely used transmission technologies in robotics.

Each technology offers unique advantages depending on the robot's requirements, including torque, precision, weight and cost.

This guide compares planetary gearboxes and harmonic drives to help engineers choose the right solution.

---

## What is a Planetary Gearbox?

A planetary gearbox consists of:

* Sun Gear
* Planet Gears
* Ring Gear
* Carrier

Planetary systems are compact, efficient and widely used in robotics and automation.

### Advantages

* High Efficiency
* Compact Design
* Lower Cost
* High Speed Capability
* Mature Manufacturing Process

### Limitations

* Higher Backlash
* Lower Precision Compared to Harmonic Drives

---

## What is a Harmonic Drive?

A harmonic drive uses:

* Circular Spline
* Flexspline
* Wave Generator

This technology is known for extremely low backlash and high positioning accuracy.

### Advantages

* Near-Zero Backlash
* High Position Accuracy
* Compact Form Factor
* High Reduction Ratios

### Limitations

* Higher Cost
* Lower Shock Resistance
* More Sensitive to Overload

## Which One Should You Choose?

Choose a planetary gearbox if:

* Cost is important
* Shock loads are expected
* High efficiency is required

Choose a harmonic drive if:

* Maximum precision is required
* Very low backlash is critical
* Budget is less constrained
