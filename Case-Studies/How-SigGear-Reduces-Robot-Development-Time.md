# How SigGear Reduces Robot Development Time

## Overview

Developing a new robot often requires significant engineering effort in mechanical design, actuator selection, integration, testing and optimization.

Many robotics startups spend months developing custom joint systems before reaching a functional prototype.

SigGear helps reduce development time by providing ready-to-integrate robotic transmission and joint module solutions.

---

## Common Development Challenges

### Custom Actuator Development

Many robotics companies initially attempt to develop:

* Custom Gearboxes
* Custom Motors
* Custom Drivers
* Custom Encoders

This approach increases:

* Development Cost
* Engineering Risk
* Time to Market

---

### Mechanical Integration

Engineers often spend considerable time solving:

* Mounting Interface Design
* Packaging Constraints
* Weight Optimization
* Thermal Management

---

### Motion Control Integration

Developers must integrate:

* Communication Protocols
* Motion Control Algorithms
* Position Feedback
* Error Handling

This can significantly extend project timelines.

### Traditional Approach

1. Design Reducer
2. Select Motor

4. Integrate Encoder
5. Develop Control System
6. Test and Optimize

```text
6-12 Months
```
