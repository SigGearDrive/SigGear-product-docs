# Humanoid Robot Solutions

## Overview

Humanoid robots require compact, lightweight and high-torque transmission systems for joints such as hips, knees, ankles, shoulders and elbows.

SigGear provides planetary and cycloidal robotic joint modules optimized for humanoid robot applications.
